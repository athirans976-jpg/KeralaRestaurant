Kerala's Culinary Soul

Nestled in the backwaters of Alleppey and the hills of Munnar, our cuisine celebrates coconut, fresh spices, and the bounty of the Arabian Sea. At Malabar Spice, we recreate the authentic flavors of a traditional Kerala thali or Sadya served on a banana leaf with over 20 elements.

Our signature dishes include Karimeen Pollichathu (pearl spot fish wrapped in banana leaf), Kerala Parotta with Beef Roast, and Elaneer Payasam (tender coconut pudding). Every dish is slow cooked with our homemade spice blends and local ingredients.
